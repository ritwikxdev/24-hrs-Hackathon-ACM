Types of Fraud Detected

📈

Upcoding

Billing for a more expensive service or procedure than what was actually provided to the patient to maximize payout.

📑

Duplicate Claims

Submitting multiple claims for the same service, same patient, and same date, either accidentally or intentionally.

👻

Ghost Billing

Submitting claims for services that were never performed, or billing for patients who never visited the hospital.
